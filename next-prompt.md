A premium, cinematic landing page for the "Touta's World" educational brand. The hero artwork must be an actual image element (NOT a background image) with soft, rounded styling, positioned next to elegant, editorial typography.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first
- Theme: Ethereal, sophisticated, and deeply nostalgic
- Philosophy: Balances playful innocence with high-end editorial elegance. Relies heavily on generous whitespace and cinematic proportions.
- Background: Canvas White (#FAFAFA)
- Surface/Cards: Parchment Cream (#FDFBF7)
- Primary Text: Midnight Charcoal (#1A1A1A) with Serif for headings, Sans for body
- Accents: Terracotta Glow (#E27D60) for buttons; Sunlit Olive (#C2C384), Golden Sand (#E8A87C) for organic decorative elements
- Eyebrows: Overlines (text-xs, uppercase, tracking-[0.3em]) inside a soft-edged container
- Buttons: Pill-shaped (rounded-full), solid Terracotta background with a soft, diffused shadow (shadow-xl)
- Images/Containers: Generously rounded (rounded-3xl or rounded-[2rem])

**Page Structure:**
1. **Header:** A floating, glassmorphism navigation bar with a centered "Touta" logo and clean secondary links. Soft shadow underneath.
2. **Hero Section (Asymmetrical Layout):** Left side: A clean editorial heading "Begin your journey with Touta" with an elegant eyebrow line above it ("Welcome to the magic") and the primary "Start Exploring" CTA below. Right side: The main Pyramids artwork placed as a large, beautiful image with rounded corners (`rounded-3xl`) and shadow, NOT as a full-screen background.
3. **Mascot Introduction:** A very spacious section. Left side: The Touta mascot image. Right side: A gentle greeting and a paragraph explaining Touta's role as a storytelling companion.
4. **Product Exhibits:** A gallery-style row displaying four primary items (Notebooks, Puzzles, Pins, Coloring Books). Each product is isolated on a soft canvas background.
5. **Footer:** Simple, elegant links with social icons and a copyright line.
